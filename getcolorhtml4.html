How To Play:

In the Game interface, Some color buttons will appear on the screen and also a Get (White, Lime, Violet, Indigo, Blue, green, Yellow, Orange, Red) window will appear on the screen with changing names of the colors in every 2 seconds.

Time decrease by 200 milli seconds with after each 5 successful gettings to increase dificulty.

You got three lifes in the game.

The Chalange of the game is to click on the same color button as soon as before the change in color name in Get Color Window.

If you are unable to select the color button before it change, You will lose a life.

If you lose all your three lifes, the game will over.
